## Explain
### Slogan
- Gratis. Obligation less. Jobs done without expecting any single monetary rewards.Software products shall be given for $0 & for Hardware shall be priced appropriately, affordably.
- Open Source. Transparent. Jobs done as clear as it is (honesty), be let known for how it's done, and even be let it forc, hacc, pull request. From the core to the final product the knowledge & schematic codings shall be public, visible to everyone for people to analyze, learn, & spread. From the main element to all contributor shall be acknowledged
- **FULL VERSION!!!**. Passionate. Jobs done as best as it can be, meticulously. Product & Service shall be served at full out of the box, and if modular, then recommend essential modules with the main product.
